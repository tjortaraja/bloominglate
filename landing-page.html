A letter to myself

Dear future me,

You are not late. You are arriving with weather in your voice, proof in your hands, and a softer kind of certainty than the one you used to chase.

Some beginnings waited until you had the language for them. Let that be part of the story, not an apology for it.

Bloom anyway. Start anyway. Let the life you meant to make find you here.
