Brand guidelines

For people arriving at important things later than planned.

A calm, grown-up identity for reflection, personal essays, and practical tools that help people begin again without apology.

01 / Essence

Quietly defiant, never apologetic.

Brand Idea

Blooming Late is about the dignity of arriving with more life in you. It is reflective, assured, warm, and quietly defiant.

What It Is Not

Not hustle culture. Not a fix-yourself project. Not a cute floral metaphor. The brand makes room for timing, complexity, and renewal.

02 / Brand World

The recurring ideas Blooming Late returns to.

05 / Iconography

A small symbol language built from the logo's geometry.

Reference

Use Phosphor Icons as the functional reference for categories, clarity, and weight discipline, but redraw core brand icons in the Blooming Late geometry.

Geometry

Rounded corners, quarter-circle curves, simple construction, open interiors, and consistent stroke widths. Icons should feel related to the B mark.

Weights

Regular by default, thin for editorial moments, bold for tracker navigation and emphasis. Avoid filled variants except special UI states.

09 / Imagery

Field notes, real life, quiet wisdom.

Inspiration board

Visual Direction

Botanical field-guide drawings, coastal notes, moonlit paths, letter-writing objects, and archival studies. The feeling is observed, collected, and quietly alive.

Drawing Style

Fine black ink, engraved hatching, realistic natural forms, transparent backgrounds, no labels, no text, and no frames inside the artwork.

Colour Ratio

Use mostly black ink, a little Deep Moss, and rare amber highlights. The accent should feel discovered, not decorated.

Use For

Editorial essays, letter pages, product covers, tracker collections, seasonal prompts, social cards, and quiet moments of emotional orientation.

Library Rule

Botanical specimens should show the full tree, plant, or vine wherever possible. Landscapes should be unframed vignettes. Artifacts may appear with botanicals, and the clean notebook and letter variants should stay botanical-free.

Asset Format

Use transparent PNGs with no paper texture, no labels, no readable writing, no borders, and no cast shadow. Coastal scenes should use Swahili dhow boats rather than generic sailboats.

11 / Voice

Intimate, clear, and grown-up.

Voice Foundation

Honest, tender without being sentimental, encouraging without being noisy, reflective without being vague.

NVC Lens

Write from observation, feeling, need, and invitation. Name what is true without blame, diagnosis, fixing, or pressure.

Do

  • Name what is observable before interpreting it.
  • Use warm, plain language with emotional precision.
  • Make invitations instead of demands.
  • Leave room for complexity, grief, humour, and relief.
  • Speak to the reader as capable, not broken.

Don't

  • Shame people for being late, behind, messy, or unsure.
  • Use hustle language, urgency, or countdown pressure.
  • Diagnose the reader or turn their life into a problem to fix.
  • Over-soften into vague comfort or cute floral language.
  • Promise transformation that sounds instant or absolute.
